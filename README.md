# AI Systems Architecture Notes

Practical notes on designing production-grade AI systems.

## What this is

Short, opinionated notes on the gap between AI prototypes and production AI systems. Covers orchestration, observability, reliability, cost control, and the architectural decisions that matter when real users hit your system.

## Who it's for

Backend and AI engineers building LLM-powered products. Technical founders and CTOs evaluating AI system designs. Anyone moving past the "it works in a demo" stage.

## Topics

- Prototypes vs production systems
- LLM orchestration and workflow design
- Observability and tracing for AI systems
- Evaluation and quality measurement
- RAG and context design
- Agent architecture and state management
- Reliability, fallbacks, and cost control
